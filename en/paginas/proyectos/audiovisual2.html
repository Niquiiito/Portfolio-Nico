Audiovisual Proyects

Taller Ramirez

Luis Tomás "Chiquito" Ramírez, aged 85, possesses an inexhaustible vitality. Clinging to his traditions and work, he spends his days much as he has for the past 65 years. An unforeseen and sorrowful event has confined him to a nostalgic routine, allowing him to only coexist with his memories (original music). In this context, I had the honor of composing the music.

14 mins 40 secs

Bao

On this occasion, I have chosen Pixar's short film "BAO" to carry out my own interpretation, modifying both the original music and sound effects. "BAO" tells the significant preparation a mother undertakes as her son leaves, a touching theme. This brief seven-minute short film has been the core of my university thesis.

7 mins 45 secs

The Visit

This short film from Netflix's "Love, Death & Robots" series immediately captured my attention and I liked it so much that I undertook the ambitious task of recreating the entire soundtrack, with the purpose of infusing it with my personal style.

2 mins 10 secs

Luna Park

"Luna Park" is an original series from Netflix that sets its plot in 1960s Rome. In this story, a free-spirited young woman, raised within the confines of a family circus, finds herself unexpectedly confronted with the revelation that she belongs to two radically different worlds. In this context, I decided to recreate the outro of the episodes, with the intention of capturing the main essence of the series.

4 mins 21 secs

Contracciónes

The monologues of two pregnant women, developed in two different times but united by the same circumstances of gestation, slowly weave the story of a mother and her daughter born in captivity. The symmetry and knots between both dialogues reveal the true identity. My contribution to this project focuses on sound editing.

50 mins

Emergentes

"Emergentes" is a web documentary series that delves into the work of various rapidly rising alternative artists; each episode delves into the work and field of different creators. My contribution to this project focuses on sound editing.

2 mins 10 secs

Sancor

En esta ocasión, me propuse recrear el branding de un producto de la empresa Sancor, específicamente "Sancor Bebé 3". Esta tarea ha involucrado la composición de música original, la inclusión del audiologo distintivo y la meticulosa elaboración de todos los efectos de sonido requeridos para el proyecto.

1 min 26 segs
