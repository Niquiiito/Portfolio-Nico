Original Music and SFX for VideoGames

Lake Ridden

Lake Ridden is an exciting adventure with supernatural elements that combines puzzles and first-person exploration. Lake Ridden immerses the player in an atmospheric story where they investigate the disappearance of the protagonist's sister. My goal here was to recreate the entire soundtrack to deepen the game's main theme.

Street Fighter II

In this case, I decided to embark on a creative exercise by creating original music with an 8-bit style. To do this, I immersed myself in the essence of 8-bit arcade game music, which refers to an electronic music genre inspired by the sound of old computers and consoles from the 8-bit video game era. This music often captures the sounds of technology considered primitive, such as the Game Boy and homemade synthesizers.

1 min 18 secs

Avengers Infinity War - Sound Re-Design

In my attempt to recreate the epic battle of "Avengers: Infinity War," I delved deeply into the world of sound design and special effects. I made every effort to select the most impactful sounds and mix them in a way that every punch and explosion resonated with power. My aim was to achieve a sound experience as thrilling and impactful as the original scene.

49 secs

God of War III

This game, centered around a demigod pursuing the destruction of the gods, was one of the games of my childhood and one of the reasons that sparked my interest in video games. As a result of this connection, I made the decision to recreate the entire soundtrack of a segment of this game.
