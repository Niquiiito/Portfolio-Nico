Nicolás Pasik

He was born in Buenos Aires in 1999 and obtained his Bachelor's degree in Film Music, as well as a Diploma in Music Production, both from the Universidad Católica Argentina (UCA). Currently, he is pursuing his studies in Orchestral Conducting at the National Conservatory Manuel de Falla.

His initiation into the world of music was intimately connected to the audiovisual sphere.

He has served as the composer of original music for multiple projects that have been featured in prestigious international festivals. Additionally, he has been involved in the advertising field. Currently, he works as a session musician in various musical projects.

He has devoted time to training in various instruments, including the piano, bass guitar, saxophone, guitar, violin, and drums.
